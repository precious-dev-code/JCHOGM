Welcome To Our Church

Jesus Christ Hope of Glory Ministries is a Christ-centered ministry devoted to exalting Christ Jesus, evangelizing the lost, encouraging the hurting, enriching the community, and equipping believers for spiritual growth. Our mission is restoring hope to the hopeless and transforming the total person through the preaching of the true and full Gospel of Christ.

Our Vision

To restore hope to the hopeless and transform the total man by preaching the true and full gospel of Christ, equipping saints, and building the church where Christ is Lord.

Our Mission

  • Exalt Christ Jesus
  • Evangelize the lost
  • Encourage the hurting
  • Enrich the community
  • Equip believers
  • Transform lives through God's Word
